Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-22

Plain English

This site is a personal portfolio. It collects the minimum necessary to function and to understand how visitors arrive. Nothing is sold, nothing is shared with advertisers.

1. Data collected directly by the site

  • Contact form submissions. When you submit the contact form, the data you provide (first name, last name, email, subject, message) is sent via the Resend email API to info@ibaifernandez.com. It is not stored on this site or in any database. Resend's privacy policy applies to its handling: resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Anti-spam telemetry. To detect bots, the contact form records a timestamp when the page is loaded and includes it in the submission. It also uses Cloudflare Turnstile, which may load a small token from challenges.cloudflare.com. Turnstile's data handling: developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile.
  • Language preference. If you toggle the language, your choice is saved in localStorage on your device under the key portfolio_language. It never leaves your browser.
  • Consent decision. If you respond to the cookie banner, your choice is saved in localStorage under the key portfolio_consent. It never leaves your browser.

2. Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (property G-T8FTTWBQS3) to understand how visitors arrive and what they read. Analytics is loaded with consent denied by default using Google Consent Mode v2. Nothing is sent to Google until you accept analytics via the consent banner.

If you accept:

  • GA4 sets cookies _ga and _ga_T8FTTWBQS3.
  • Your IP is anonymized before processing (anonymize_ip: true).
  • Ad personalization, ad storage, and ad user data remain denied.

If you decline (or never respond): GA4 is loaded but never fires. No cookies are set. No data is sent.

Change your mind: clear site data in your browser, or open the page in a private window. The consent banner re-appears.

3. Third parties

  • Netlify — hosting CDN and the contact serverless function. Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, requested path) are retained per Netlify's policy: netlify.com/privacy.
  • Resend — email delivery for contact form messages.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — bot detection on the contact form.
  • Google Fonts — Roboto webfont, loaded from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log the request. Loaded only when needed by the page.
  • Google Analytics 4 — see section 2.
  • YouTube — embedded only on the Elm St dossier page, in privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com). YouTube cookies fire only after you press play.

4. Your rights (GDPR / Chile Ley 21.719)

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is processed about you (this page).
  • Request access to or correction of your contact form submissions.
  • Request deletion of your contact form data.
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data-protection authority.

Contact: info@ibaifernandez.com.

5. Data controller

The data controller is Ibai Fernández, the site owner. Contact via info@ibaifernandez.com.

6. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The full version history is in this repository's git log.
