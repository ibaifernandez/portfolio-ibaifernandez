Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Plain English

This site is a personal portfolio. It collects the minimum necessary to function and to understand how visitors arrive. Nothing is sold, nothing is shared with advertisers. Webfonts are served from this domain — no third-party tracking from the moment the page loads.

1. Data collected directly by the site

  • Contact form submissions. When you submit the contact form, the data you provide (first name, last name, email, subject, message) is sent via the Resend email API to info@ibaifernandez.com. It is not stored on this site or in any database. Resend's privacy policy applies to its handling: resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Anti-spam telemetry. To detect bots, the contact form records a timestamp when the page is loaded and includes it in the submission. It also uses Cloudflare Turnstile, which may load a small token from challenges.cloudflare.com. Turnstile's data handling: developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile.
  • Language preference. If you toggle the language, your choice is saved in localStorage on your device under the key portfolio_language. It never leaves your browser.
  • Consent decision. If you respond to the cookie banner, your choice is saved in localStorage under the key portfolio_consent. It never leaves your browser.

2. Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (property G-T8FTTWBQS3) to understand how visitors arrive and what they read. Analytics is loaded with consent denied by default using Google Consent Mode v2. Nothing is sent to Google until you accept analytics via the consent banner.

If you accept:

  • GA4 sets cookies _ga and _ga_T8FTTWBQS3.
  • Your IP is anonymized before processing (anonymize_ip: true).
  • Ad personalization, ad storage, and ad user data remain denied.

If you decline (or never respond): GA4 is loaded but never fires. No cookies are set. No data is sent.

Change your mind at any time: use the "Cookie preferences" link in the page footer to re-open the banner and update your choice.

3. Third parties

  • Netlify — hosting CDN and the contact serverless function. Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, requested path) are retained per Netlify's policy: netlify.com/privacy.
  • Resend — email delivery for contact form messages.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — bot detection on the contact form.
  • Google Analytics 4 — see section 2. Loaded only after explicit consent.
  • YouTube — embedded only on the Elm St dossier page, in privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com). YouTube cookies fire only after you press play.

Webfonts (Roboto, Inter, Josefin Sans, Roboto Mono) are self-hosted from this domain. Google Fonts is not used. Individual dossier pages may load page-specific webfonts from their providers — disclosed at the top of each dossier page when applicable.

4. Your rights

Depending on where you reside, you may have rights under one or more of the following frameworks:

  • European Union / EEA / UK — General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR.
  • Chile — Ley 21.719 sobre Protección de Datos Personales.
  • Brasil — Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD, Lei nº 13.709/2018).
  • México — Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares (LFPDPPP).
  • Other jurisdictions — equivalent local data-protection law may apply.

Under these frameworks you generally have the right to:

  • Know what data is processed about you (this page).
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your contact form submissions.
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time — use the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Request data portability where applicable.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data-protection authority.

How to exercise your rights (Data Subject Request)

Email info@ibaifernandez.com with subject "Data Subject Request" and describe what you want (access, correction, deletion, etc.). I will respond within 30 calendar days. No proof of identity is required for simple deletion requests; access/portability requests may ask you to confirm the email address used in the original submission.

5. Data controller

The data controller is Ibai Fernández, the site owner. Contact via info@ibaifernandez.com. There is no joint controller and no designated data protection officer (DPO) — the site does not meet the processing thresholds that would require one under GDPR Art. 37 or equivalent.

6. International data transfers

Some processors (Resend, Netlify, Cloudflare, Google) are headquartered in the United States and may process data outside your country of residence. Where required, these providers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent transfer mechanisms. By using the contact form or accepting analytics, you acknowledge these transfers.

7. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The full version history is in this repository's git log.
