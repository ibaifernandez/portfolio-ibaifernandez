About Ibai Fernández

More than two decades shipping narrative, marketing, and code.

Who I Am

Ibai Fernández. AI Product Engineer. Spaniard, EU citizen, remote from Belo Horizonte. I run AGLAYA — The Uncomfortable Agenc·AI, where I craft systems that free entrepreneurs from traditional SaaS lock-in and turn their investments into long-lasting assets. I move between narrative, marketing, and engineering; most of what I take on today demands all three at once.

What I Do

I ship systems to production. Right now: Scanner 21.719, which audits Chilean websites against Ley 21.719; multi-tenant SaaS like AGLAYA Kanban Desk; AI-driven email marketing automation infrastructure built to scale; and websites with the most solid architecture.

How I Work

Remote, async, end-to-end ownership. No throw-it-over-the-wall handoffs, no half-built scopes, no specs that die in production. Surfaces sometimes small, sometimes not so small, but always defensible, documented, tested, with CI deploy gates as a recurring part of the work.

How I Build

I architect, design, and develop the product. AI assists me in stacking the bricks. Orchestration through Claude and Codex on Node, Python… or whatever framework each deploy needs: vanilla, Astro, Flask, React; static or serverless as required. CSP-enforced, hash-pinned, with high-performance budgets in CI. The stack stays boring on purpose; the work above it… most interesting.
